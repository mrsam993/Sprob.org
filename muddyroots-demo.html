Muddyroots Demo

Although I am quite proud of this game it is hard and due to the limits of Construct 2, the controls tend to be awkward at times. I would recommend playing on no fail mode to get the full experience and to no lose within seconds. When harvesting the crops the top of each sprite need to be within the lattice of the fence so if you are to early or late it won't register. Good luck, have fun and please excuse any bugs, as this was a GameJam game and there are bound to be some.
